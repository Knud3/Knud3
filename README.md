# Moi!

I have a lifetime passion for technology, driven by a curiosity to understand how things truly work and master them. I'm a problem-solver at heart, motivated by the challenge of learning new things and applying that knowledge to complex projects. I find immense reward in seeing end-users happy, their problems solved, and especially when automation saves countless hours, hassle, and potential troubles.

I'm a firm believer in automation and scripting. Whether utilizing <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/bash/bash-original.svg" alt="Bash" height="15"> **Bash**, <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/powershell/powershell-original.svg" alt="PowerShell" height="15"> **PowerShell**, or other tools, manual repetition of tasks represents a missed opportunity to engineer robust, secure and scalable solutions.

I am at home with any OS, from <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/linux/linux-original.svg" alt="Linux" height="15"> **Linux** and <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/apple/apple-original.svg" alt="macOS" height="15"> **macOS** to <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/windows11/windows11-original.svg" alt="Windows" height="15"> **Windows**, provided it allows for modification and tinkering. This passion extends to the hardware level, with a lifetime hobby in building custom PCs, servers, and designing extreme liquid cooling solutions.

I enjoy architecting and building systems from the ground up. Some examples include:

- Deploying fully automated monitoring stack on <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/kubernetes/kubernetes-plain.svg" alt="Kubernetes" height="15"> **Kubernetes**, leveraging the full Grafana ecosystem, <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/grafana/grafana-original.svg" alt="Grafana" height="15"> **Grafana**, **Mimir**, **Loki**...
- Building zero-touch deployment systems for workstations using **WDS/MDT**
- Automating the creation and deployment of up-to-date <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/vsphere/vsphere-original.svg" alt="VMware" height="15"> **VMware** templates with <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/powershell/powershell-original.svg" alt="PowerShell" height="15"> **PowerShell** scripts
- Implementing IaC (Infrastructure as Code) for infrastructure automation, managing configurations, and deploying applications using tools like <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/terraform/terraform-original.svg" alt="Terraform" height="15"> **Terraform**, <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/ansible/ansible-original.svg" alt="Ansible" height="15"> **Ansible**, <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/helm/helm-original.svg" alt="Helm" height="15"> **Helm**, **Kustomize**, and <img src="https://skaffold.dev/images/skaffold-logo-white.png" alt="Skaffold" height="15"> **Skaffold**

My interests also extend to home lab setups and self-hosting:

- I explore virtualization with <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/proxmox/proxmox-original-wordmark.svg" alt="Proxmox" height="15"> **Proxmox**
- Advanced networking with <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/devicons/devicon/master/icons/pfsense/pfsense-original.svg" alt="pfSense" height="15"> **pfSense Plus** and <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/mikrotik/002B4C" alt="MikroTik" height="15"> **MikroTik**, and expose services via <img src="https://www.vectorlogo.zone/logos/wireguard/wireguard-icon.svg" alt="WireGuard" height="15"> **WireGuard** and <img src="https://images.icon-icons.com/2699/PNG/512/haproxy_logo_icon_171017.png" alt="HAProxy" height="15"> **HAProxy**
- For storage, I use <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/truenas/0095D5" alt="TrueNAS" height="15"> **TrueNAS** and <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/syncthing/2296F3" alt="Syncthing" height="15"> **Syncthing** for decentralized file sync, including offsite backups
- And self-hosting services like <img src="https://cdn.simpleicons.org/homeassistant/049CD0" alt="Home Assistant" height="15"> **Home Assistant** and <img src="https://www.svgrepo.com/show/342019/matrix.svg" alt="Matrix" height="15"> **Matrix** homeserver

I'm a strong advocate for **Open-Source**. Currently immersing myself in the realm of **AI**.
